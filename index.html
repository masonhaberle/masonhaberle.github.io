About Me

I am a PhD student in the math department at the Courant Institute at New York University. In December 2020, I graduated from UC Berkeley with a Bachelors of Arts in mathematics. My focus is on computational PDEs, optimization, and dynamics for plasmas. In particular, I am working on stellarator optimization with Alan Kaptanoglu. I am also interested in turbulence theory and have done work in mathematical fluid dynamics and convex integration in the past.

On this site, I have posted some of my papers, posters, and slidetalks for public viewing.
