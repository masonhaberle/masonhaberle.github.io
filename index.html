About Me

I am a PhD student in the math department at the Courant Institute at New York University. In December 2020, I graduated from UC Berkeley with a Bachelors of Arts in mathematics. My focus is on partial differential equations for fluid dynamics and plasmas. In particular, I am interested in turubulence theory and am learning the basics of stellarator optimization.

On this site, I have posted some of my preprints and slidetalks for public viewing.
